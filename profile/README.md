## Welcome to the GerLab maker community

**GerLab** is a FabLab and creative fabrication space in Mongolia where ideas become real through making, testing, learning, and sharing.

We bring together digital fabrication, engineering, science, design, education, art, and Mongolian maker culture. Our work is built around one simple idea:

> **Learning by doing.**

This GitHub organization is where GerLab collects and shares open maker knowledge: projects, machine guides, learning resources, material notes, safety practices, event records, and future handbook content.

GerLab is not only a room with machines.  
It is a place where people sketch, model, print, cut, code, build, break, improve, document, and share.

## What GerLab Stands For

GerLab exists to grow a creative maker environment in Mongolia where students, engineers, artists, researchers, educators, and makers can turn ideas into physical things.

We make first, learn deeply, document what works, share what fails, and help the next maker build faster.

**Made with care in Mongolia.**
