# GerLab  
## FabLab Mongolia · Making, Learning & Sharing

Welcome to **GerLab**, a FabLab and creative fabrication space in Mongolia.

GerLab is a place for making, experimenting, prototyping, learning, and sharing. We bring together digital fabrication, engineering, science, design, education, art, and Mongolian maker culture.

We believe in one simple idea:

> **Learning by doing.**

At GerLab, ideas are not only discussed.  
They are sketched, modeled, printed, cut, tested, broken, improved, and shared.

GerLab is not only a room with machines.  
It is a creative community where people build objects, skills, confidence, and knowledge together.

## What We Make

GerLab supports many kinds of creative and technical work:

- Digital fabrication projects
- Student prototypes
- Science education tools
- Engineering experiments
- Art and design objects
- Electronics and Arduino projects
- 3D printed models
- Laser-cut objects and patterns
- CNC and woodworking projects
- Mongolian culture-inspired creations
- Community workshop projects

---

## Mission

GerLab’s mission is to grow a creative maker environment in Mongolia where people can turn ideas into real objects through hands-on learning, collaboration, and open knowledge.

We support students, engineers, artists, researchers, educators, and makers who want to create, test, fail, improve, and share their work.

---

## Values

- **Make, test, improve** — We learn by creating real things.
- **Share knowledge** — We document projects, files, settings, mistakes, and fixes.
- **Create with curiosity** — We explore science, engineering, design, art, education, and culture.
- **Respect the workshop** — We care for tools, machines, materials, people, and space.
- **Build with Mongolian identity** — We connect modern fabrication with local culture, patterns, materials, and needs.
- **Work safely** — Safety protects the freedom to keep making.
